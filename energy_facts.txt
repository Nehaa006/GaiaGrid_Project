The Spanish electricity market clearing price is highly volatile and affected by weather conditions. 
High temperatures above 30 degrees Celsius increase demand due to air conditioning. 
Solar energy production peaks at midday, often lowering prices. 
Charging the battery is most cost-effective during off-peak hours (typically 2 AM to 6 AM). 
Discharging should occur during evening peak hours (7 PM to 10 PM) for maximum ROI.
